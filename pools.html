Sterns Mission Beach

This Pool is made from a blue scope zinclum colorbond wall and BHP Duragal powder-coated frame.
They are a freshwater pool, braced 1.2 metres deep. Round Pools how ever are braceless.
Flat floor option available.

The Mission Beach is priced to the budget conscious but with a long lasting life.
Available in a limited range of round and oval sizes and has a 100mm wide top coping with sleep look verticals and a fully made Australian Steel Frame.

Sterns Lindeman

This Sterns Pool is a Robust Braceless Pool with heavy duty 175mm top decks.
It is available in a range of oval and round shapes - flat floor only.
At 1.2 metres deep it is a freshwater pool made from a Bluescope Zincalum wall and BHP Duragal braceless frame. It is built tough.
‍
Liners are .50mm thick in sky or royal blue but patterned options are available.
This Pool has been in existence for over 30 years and has a proven track record of long life.

Sterns Whitsunday

This pool features wide 200mm resin top decks and vertical posts with marine grade 316 stainless steel fixings. It is a fully blown salt water pool, engineered with resin parts that are highly resistant to corrosion from the salty water.

The Pool is available in the full range of Sterns shapes and sizes and is 1.37 metre high.

Available in Round, Oval, Keyhole, Raindrop, and Rectangular both flat floor and deep end options. The largest range in the southern hemisphere supplied with an extra heavy duty 0.50mm thick linder for long life and durability
