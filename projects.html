Projects

School and Commercial

Here at Poolmaster Pools we can make custom Pools for Swim Schools and indoor gymnasiums.
The widths are 2.55, 3.15, 3.8, 4.56 and 5.76 metres.

The shapes are oval for all sizes plus in the 3.8 metre width rectangular as well.
The maximum length we can go is between 24 and 25 metres.
The benefit of a modular pool built inside a unit is that it is never permanent like an in-ground pool.
The frame sits flat on the concrete floor of the unit.
